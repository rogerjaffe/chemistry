Mass, Density and Volume-Word Problems

This page presents random "word" problems in the areas of mass, density and volume. When you press "New Question", a question will appear to the right of the tables. Calculate the answer, enter it in the answer cell and press "Check Answer". The results appear in the table. If you get a problem wrong, you should redo it until you get it correct, but you can press "New Question" at any time.
  • If you miss a problem three times, pressing the "Show Answer" button will cause a complete solution to appear.
