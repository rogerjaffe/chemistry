Graham's Law

This page provides work in the use of Graham's Law of Effusion. Its operation is very straightforward. When you press "New Problem", a question will be displayed to the right of the table. The question may require that you do a calculation and enter a numeric value or it may be multiple choice. Determine the correct response and submit your answer. Resulting and scoring are kept on the main window.
  • If you miss a problem three times, pressing the "Show Answer" button will cause the correct answer to appear.
