Volume, mass and moles-relating two elements

On this page you will be asked to use basic information provided about a sample of an element to determine a property of a sample of a different element. To answer the questions you will need a periodic table which includes the densities of the elements.

Using the page

  • To obtain a question, press the "New Question" button and a question appears in the top frame, along with an empty cell and a "Check Answer" Button.
  • Enter your answer in the cell in the top frame and press "Check Answer."
  • Results will be displayed in the table on the main frame.
  • If you miss a problem three times, pressing "Show Answer" will a complete solution to appear.
