Converting Base 10 and Binary Numbers

This page is an exercise in converting Base 10 (decimal) numbers and binary numbers. When you press "New Problem", one of the cells will fill. Put the corresponding value in the empty cell and press "Check Answer". The results will appear in the second table.
Hints
  • If you get a question incorrect, you should redo it and resubmit the answer.
  • Do not use "leading zeros". That is, do not input binary numbers like "001101".
