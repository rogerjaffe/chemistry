Faraday's Laws-Homework

This is an exercise in electrolytic processes. When you press "New Question", an unbalanced redox process with a question will appear in the top frame. Determine the value of the answer, enter it in the cell and press "Check Answer." Results appear in the second table. Be careful about the dimensions, the cathode/anode and the direction of spontaneity. You need a table of standard potentials to answer the question and you can assume all molarities and pressures are at unity.
