Density and Moles Word Problems-Homework

Density,mass, moles and multiple elements

This page presents random "word" problems in the area of elements, density, mass and moles. When you press "New Question", a question will appear in the top frame. Calculate the answer and enter it in the cell. Then, press "Check Answer" and the results appear in the table. If you get a problem wrong, you should redo it until you get it correct, but you can press "New Question" at any time. You need a periodic table or chart of the elements which includes densities to do these problems. Be very careful about the atomic symbol. You must enter it exactly as is shown on the Periodic Table.
