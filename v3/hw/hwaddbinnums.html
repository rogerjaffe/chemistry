Adding Binary Numbers

Here you can practice binary addition. When you press "New Problem," binary numbers will appear in the first two cells. Enter their sum as a binary number in the third cell and press "Check Answer." Results appear in the second table. Hints
  • If you get a question incorrect, you should redo it and resubmit the answer.
  • Do not use "leading zeros". That is, do not input binary numbers like "001101".
