Converting Distance

This is a drill and practice page involving converting distance measures between the English (inches, feet and yards) and metric (mm,cm,meters,km). It is intended to help with both your math skill and your understanding of the metric system.

Hints

  • First, select and submit the instructor's name.
  • To begin-press "New Problem" and three of the four cells will fill.
  • Put the correct value into the empty cell and press "Check Answer."
  • The results on the problem and a running total will appear in the second table.
  • If you get a problem "incorrect", you should redo it and recheck your answer.
