Calculating Eo

This is an exercise in calculating standard potential. When you press "New Reaction", an unbalanced redox process will appear in the top frame. Use a table of standard half potentials to determine the overall cell potential, enter it in the cell and press "Check Answer." If you wish, you may press the "Show Eos" button which will provide a table of the Eos used on this page. Results appear in the second table. Be careful about the sign of the potential and enter it with at least .01V precision.
