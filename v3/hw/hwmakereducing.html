Generating Reducible Representations

This page will present problems involving the production of reducible representations. When you press "New Problem", you will first be asked to identify the point group for a particular species. Once that is done correctly, a problem involving a particular reducible representation and a response table will be presented in the top frame. Enter the correct characters in the table and press the "Submit" button. Results appear in the main frame. Before beginning, enter your name, select your instructor and press "Submit."
